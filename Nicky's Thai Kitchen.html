Welcome to Nicky's Thai Kitchen

It is our pleasure to bring the best of traditional Thai cuisine to the Pittsburgh region for both Dine in and Take Away.

We Want to THANK YOU for voting us Best Thai Restaurant in the City Paper Readers Poll!

 

We offer both lunch and dinner.
Dining reservations are available for dinner.

You are welcome to bring your own Alcohol*

*Northside location corkage fee is $5 per bottle.

*The Downtown location has a bar with a selection of wines, sakes, unique cocktails and beer, including the refreshing Singha Beer from Thailand.  Corkage fee downtown is $10 per bottle, $2 per beer.
